ABES ENGINEERING COLLEGE

ABES Engineering College is a premier institution located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh. It is known for its excellent academic environment and modern infrastructure. The college offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and management. With experienced faculty and active industry collaborations, students receive quality education and practical exposure. The campus also provides vibrant extracurricular opportunities for holistic development.
